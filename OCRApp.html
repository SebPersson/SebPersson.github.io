Idea

The idea of challenging your body and mind by running an Obstacle Course Race have in recent years risen rapidly in popularity as everything from an individual training goal to a teambuilding excercise.

Purpose

The idea is to create an app for people who are new to, or are interested in, OCR in general and want easy-to-read and easy-to-find information.

Features

  • The app provides information about the different obstalces, what the obstacles require and different techniques to overcome them.
  • The app includes different workouts aimed towards OCR.
  • The app includes a bunch of different "tricks-of-the-trade" from experienced runners conserning everything from preparations to what gear to use
  • The app will feature videos and information from people of different experience ranging from beginners to happy amateurs to OCR elite runners.
  • The app is developed in cooperation with gyms oriented toward Calisthenics, OCR and Ninja Warrior.

    Development

    The app is currently under development. The first beta-version will be available for download on this webpage when its done. Feedback and new ideas will be appreciated.
